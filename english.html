About the Company

Mualo Aluminum Carpentry is an Angolan company, specialized in high-quality aluminum solutions for homes, businesses, and industries.

With a team of experienced professionals, we offer manufacturing, installation, and maintenance services for doors, windows, aluminum facades, and much more.

Our commitment is to excellence, durability, and complete customer satisfaction, using top-quality materials and modern manufacturing techniques.
