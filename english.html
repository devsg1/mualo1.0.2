About the Company

Mualo Aluminum Carpentry is an Angolan company specialized in high-quality aluminum solutions for residences, businesses and industries.

With a team of experienced professionals, we offer manufacturing, installation and maintenance services for aluminum doors, windows, facades and much more.

Our commitment is to excellence, durability and total customer satisfaction, using first-class materials and modern manufacturing techniques.

Area of Activity

Geographic Coverage

We operate throughout the province of Luanda, with capacity for projects in other provinces of Angola, ensuring quality and punctuality in all regions.
